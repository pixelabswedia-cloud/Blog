Privacy Policy

Published on January 1, 0001

At Tech Insights Daily, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by our website and how it is used.

Log Files Like many other Web sites, we make use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks.

Cookies and Web Beacons We use cookies to store information about visitors’ preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user accesses or visits, and customize Web page content based on visitors’ browser type.

Google AdSense Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.
